Chapter 1: A Quiet Morning

The sun rose slowly over the hills, casting a soft golden light across the small village.

Liam stood by the window, holding a cup of warm coffee. Everything seemed peaceful, but deep inside, something felt wrong.

For three nights in a row, he had the same dream. A distant voice calling his name from the darkness.

"Today feels different," he whispered.

And somehow, he knew everything was about to change.


Chapter 2: The Dream Returns

That night, Liam couldn’t sleep.

As soon as he closed his eyes, the dream came back.

He stood in a vast empty field. The sky was dark, but in the distance, a single light flickered.

"Liam..." the voice called again.

This time, it was clearer. Closer.

He tried to move, but his body wouldn’t respond.

The light suddenly expanded, swallowing everything.

He woke up, breathing heavily.

"This isn’t just a dream," he said.


Chapter 3: The Stranger

The next morning, Liam walked into town.

People moved as usual, chatting and laughing, unaware of the unease growing inside him.

Then he saw her.

A woman in a dark coat stood in the middle of the street, looking directly at him.

"You’ve seen it, haven’t you?" she asked.

Liam froze.

"The light," she continued.

"Who are you?" Liam asked.

She smiled slightly.

"Someone who knows what’s coming."
